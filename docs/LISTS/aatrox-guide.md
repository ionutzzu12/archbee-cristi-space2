Aatrox — The Darkin Blade

Aatrox is a **Juggernaut** champion in League of Legends, the host of the Darkin blade who seeks to end all life and free himself from imprisonment.

## Overview

- **Role:** Top lane, occasionally Jungle
- **Class:** Juggernaut / Fighter
- **Resource:** Manaless (uses Health for some abilities)
- **Difficulty:** Moderate

## Playstyle

Aatrox excels in sustained combat and healing through damage. His **World Ender** ultimate and **Deathbringer Stance** passive make him a strong duelist and team-fight threat when he can land his **The Darkin Blade** (Q) sweet spots.
